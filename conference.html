Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advancements in Science, Social Science, and Technology (ICRASST) 2025 marks the inaugural edition of this interdisciplinary event, organized by St. George’s College, Aruvithura, Kottayam, Kerala, India. As a first-of-its-kind conference, ICRASST 2025 aims to bring together researchers, scholars, and professionals from diverse fields to share groundbreaking research, explore emerging trends, and foster cross-disciplinary collaboration. This edition sets the stage for a dynamic exchange of ideas, offering a platform for presenting innovative solutions and forging new connections in the rapidly evolving landscape of science, social science, and technology.
