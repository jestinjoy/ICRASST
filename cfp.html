Themes

ICRASST 2025 encourages submissions that align with, but are not limited to, the following themes:

  1. Advancements in Science and Technology
  2. Innovations in Commerce and Management
  3. Interdisciplinary Approaches to Sustainable Development
  4. Emerging Trends in Food Science and Technology
  5. Mathematical Modeling and Computational Techniques

Tracks

Papers are invited in the following specific tracks:

  1. Physics and Materials Science
  2. Chemistry and Chemical Engineering
  3. Botany and Environmental Science
  4. Mathematics and Statistics
  5. Computer Applications and IT
  6. Food Science and Technology
  7. Commerce and Management
  8. Innovation and Entrepreneurship (cross-disciplinary)
